CleanSky is a contactless payment app that lets you pay and receive cryptocurrency using NFC - just tap your phone.

YOUR KEYS, YOUR FUNDS, YOUR SOVEREIGNTY

• Self-custody wallet - you control your private keys
• No account needed - no email, no phone number, no KYC
• Works offline - sign transactions without internet
• Open source - verify, don't trust

PRIVACY BY DESIGN

• Stealth addresses (EIP-5564) - each payment goes to a unique address
• Payer privacy (ERC-4337) - hide your wallet address when paying
• No tracking, no analytics, no data collection

SIMPLE & FAST

• Tap to pay - NFC contactless payments in seconds
• Tap to collect - receive payments as a merchant
• Multi-chain support - Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base, and more
• Gasless options - pay without needing ETH for gas

BUILT FOR REAL USE

• Works with unstable currencies - protect your savings
• 70+ languages supported
• Low-end phone friendly - works on Android 7+
• No Google Services required

SECURITY

• AES-256-GCM encryption with Android Keystore
• Biometric authentication (fingerprint/face)
• StrongBox HSM support on compatible devices
• No cloud backup - your keys never leave your device

CleanSky is free, open source software. We don't have access to your funds and cannot help you recover lost keys. Always backup your private key securely.
